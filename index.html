Cup of Joe

An Alternative/Indie Baguio-based Band

About Cup of Joe

Cup of Joe is composed of six talented lads from Baguio namely vocalists Edgar Gian "Gian" Bernardino and Raphaell "Rapha" Ridao, pianist Vixen “Xen” Gareza, lead guitarist Antonio Gabriel “Gab” Fernandez, bassist Raphael “Seve” Severino and rhythm guitarist Clint Joules “CJ” Fernandez and almost all of them came from a family with musical backgrounds.

Here's a timeline of Cup of Joe's Achievements:
  • November 2018 - They met as high school students in Baguio and formed the band after graduation. The band was formed just for an intermission number at their school, which is Saint Louis University Laboratory High School-Senior High.
  • September 2018 - They decided to form the band as a real band, and they planned to release their first composed song.
  • February 2019 - They released their debut song entitled "Nag-iisang Muli. This track is all about longing for someone only to come into a realization of being alone once again."
  • April 2019 - The band released their second single, entitled "Sinderela". The song is all about a person who loves another person, and it tells that the person will not stop doing his/her best to get the person that he/she loves.
  • July 2019 - They signed a 5-year contract with Viva Records. Moreover, their song "Nag-iisang Muli", which was their debut song went viral and became a big hit, garnering half a million streams on Spotify. Additionally, it was nominated for Best Regional Song at the MOR Awards, and fortunately, they won.
  • October 2019 - They released a new song entitled "Alas Dose". It is a Christmas-themed pop love song, and based from its title, it refers to the time that marks the start of Christmas day celebration."
  • December 2019 - Cup of joe hit 744.9k streams on Spotify with 225.1k listeners from 77 countries around the world.
  • January 2020 - They released their fourth song, entitled "Sagada". This song does not only pertain to the place itself. In this song, "Sagada" represents a person who helps you escape from everything. Someone who would never leave your side, no matter what.
  • March 2020 - Their debut song, "Nag-iisang Muli" has hit 1 million streams in Spotify.
  • July 2020 - They were nominated as the New Group Recording Artist in the Awit Awards 2020. Their song "Alas Dose" was also nominated as the Best Christmas Recording in the said event.
  • September 2020 - Their song "Sagada" reached 522,055 streams on Spotify.
  • October 2020 - Their Christmas song, entitled "Alas Dose" reached over 400,000 streams on Spotify and other streaming platforms.
  • November 2020 - Cup of Joe's second single, "Sinderela" reached 500,000+ streams on Spotify."
  • November 2020 - The released a new song, entitled "Hayaan". This song is about letting go, and it also teaches us that it’s okay to be vulnerable, and that you don’t have to pretend that you’re okay, even though you’re not.
  • December 2020 - Cup of Joe reached 2.1 million streams on Spotify with 502.4k listeners from 82 countries around the world.
  • January 2021 - "Alas Dose" reached 500,000+ streams on spotify. "Sagada" also reached 800,000 streams on Spotify. Furthermore, "Hayaan" reached 100,000+ streams on Spotify.
  • March 2021 - Their first released song or their debut single, "Nag-iisang Muli" reached 2 million streams on spotify.
  • April 2021 - Cup of Joe released their new song, entitled "Bukod-Tangi". It is a song that reminds us of the beauty of being unique and different. The track is inspired by those who are afraid of showing their true selves for the fear of being judged.
  • April 2021 - Their latest song "Bukod-Tangi" was featured in three Spotify Playlist: New Music Friday Philippines, Tatak Pinoy, and OPM Rising.
  • April 2021 - "Sagada" reached 1 million streams on Spotify. Also, Cup of Joe had 100,000+ monthly listeners on Spotify.
  • May 2021 - "Bukod-Tagi" has now reached 100,000+ streams on Spotify. The song also debuted at #47 on Spotify PH Viral 50 Chart.
  • June 2021 - The Official Lyric Video of their song, "Hayaan" reached 1 million views on Youtube.
  • July 2021 - They released a new song, entitled "Ikaw Pa Rin Ang Pipiliin Ko". This song is a ballad originally written by Marion Aunor that was released in 2018. In their rendition, they decided to the the song to a different direction: from the dark piano driven ballad to a folk-pop track with upbeat drums and violin riffs. Contextually, their reformulation transformed the song from its original messages of resentment, regret, and unrequited love to a song of faith, hope, and unconditional love.
  • July 2021 - "Ikaw Pa Rin Ang Pipiliin Ko" reached 60,000+ streams on Spotify in its first 10 days. The song also become the cover of Spotify's OPM Rising Playlist, and featured on Spotify's Tatak Pinoy and OPM Says Chillax Playlists. On its first 10 days, the Official Lyric Video Visualizer also hit 100,000 views on Youtube.
  • July 2021 - In just 2 weeks, "Ikaw Pa Rin Ang Pipiliin Ko" reached 101,447 streams on Spotify.
  • August 2021 - "Bukod-Tangi" reached 500,000 streams on Spotify. Cup of Joe also reached 25,000 followers on their Spotify Artist Account. Additionally, "Ikaw Pa Rin Ang Pipiliin Ko" reached 500,000 streams on Spotify.
  • October 2021 - Cup of Joe was featured on a Spotify billboard along the busy roads of Guadalupe.
  • April 2022 - They released a new song, entitled "Mananatili". It is a feel-good song about how we should not worry too much about the uncertainty of tomorrow and just live in the moment. It shows the listeners the beauty of just living your life to the fullest and immersing yourself in it. This song is inspired by 80's disco music and gives off a vintage vibe that will leave you wanting to celebrate life.
  • April 2022 - Cup of Joe becomes the cover of Spotify's Pinoy Indie Mix Playlist.
  • May 2022 - Cup of Joe has been featured on Spotify's OPM Rising billboards in EDSA Guadalupe.
  • June 2022 - They released their latest single, entitled "Estranghero". This track is primarily about estranged relationships. People slowly loosing touch with one another, forgetting the reasons, and becoming strangers that once loved.
  • September 2022 - Cup of Joe is featured on the cover of Spotify's OPM Rising Playlist.

Our music can be seen as an expression of each member's individuality. Our name "Cup of Joe" is a slang for "A cup of coffee". Just like a cup of coffee, our music seems ordinary; but there's always that feeling when you take a sip that really calms you down and makes you think and reflect on the finer things in life - a bittersweet blend of coffee that wakes you up and gives you the strength to face the world. This is what our music is all about.

--Cup of Joe
